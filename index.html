Company Profile

Interactive Pharma provides tailored signatory and proof-reading solutions, supporting pharmaceutical companies achieve adherent commercial success.

Based in Oxford, Interactive Pharma offers an in-house team of dedicated compliance professionals, and extensive on-site training facilities.

With strong links to Oxford University, the ABPI and the NHS, Interactive Pharma is dedicated to offering our clients a personalised, face-to-face service that goes beyond the remote consultancy model offered by many of our competitors.

Technology

At Interactive Pharma, digital technology is at the heart of everything we do. We have developed a number of bespoke digital platforms to ensure we work smarter:

Managing content and workflow
We offer a bespoke platform for managing and sharing content with you, ensuring that content and progress is managed, tracked and accessible 24/7.

Reporting
Regular reporting is key to our offering. We have a mobile-enabled analyitcs platform that keeps you up-to-date with key metrics on volume, turnaround times and KPIs.

Training
Our unique training platform is online and mobile-enabled. It offers regular updates via an information feed, and monthly training advice based on your requirements.

Training

At Interactive Pharma, we offer a wide range of training, both via traditional face-to-face meetings at our offices in Oxford, online courses, and through digital bite-sized compliance alerts.

Our training programmes include:

  • Final signatory training
  • Final signatory mentoring
  • ABPI code review
  • Managing complaints
